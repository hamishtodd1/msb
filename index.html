Murder, She Bet



Or join one:



The rules:

  1. You will watch a Murder Mystery, with several suspects, all of whom may be guilty.
  2. Any player can raise suspicion of a new suspect they would like to bet on.
  3. The guilt of the suspects is determined when, and ONLY WHEN, the credits roll.
  4. With guilt determined, bets on guilty suspects are worth money, bets on innocents are worthless.


For practice: The World's Shortest Murder Mystery!
